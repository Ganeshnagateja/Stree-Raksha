Women's safety is paramount and begins with empowerment through education, enabling individuals to recognize their rights and learn self-defense. Awareness of surroundings and utilizing technology, such as safety apps, enhances personal security and provides quick access to help. Community support and advocacy for stronger protective laws are essential in fostering a safer environment for all women.

Self-service session is used to help the user chat with the virtual assistant to clear their doubts by simply giving corresponding queries.

Tracking session helps the user track their address using the contact number provided by simply clicking the show tracking button.

Help-line session is designed to inform the user about helpline numbers for their specific state, making it easier for them to contact support.
